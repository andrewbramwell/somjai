About Somjai Thai Massage

Welcome to Somjai Thai Massage, where we bring authentic Thai massage and modern therapeutic treatments to the heart of Ross-on-Wye. Our story begins with our founder, Pen, who originates from the Surin province in Thailand.

Since moving to the UK in 2011, Pen has dedicated herself to mastering the art of massage therapy. With four years of professional experience in the UK massage industry, including valuable time at Siam Thai Spa in London's Cricklewood, she has developed a deep understanding of both traditional Thai techniques and contemporary western approaches.

Pen's commitment to excellence is reflected in her impressive qualifications, including a VTCT Level 3 Certificate in Thai Massage and a Diploma in Aromatherapy Massage. As a registered member of the Guild of Holistic Therapists (GHT), she maintains the highest standards of professional practice.

Opening Somjai Thai Massage in November 2024 marks the realisation of a dream to create a wellness sanctuary that perfectly balances professional expertise with personalised care. As the sole therapist, Pen brings focused attention and consistency to every treatment, ensuring each client receives the highest quality care.

Our approach to wellness is deeply rooted in understanding each client's unique needs. Before every session, we conduct thorough consultations to create personalised treatments that address your specific concerns. Whether you're seeking stress relief, pain management, or simple relaxation, our range of services combines traditional wisdom with modern therapeutic techniques.

At Somjai Thai Massage, we pride ourselves on maintaining exceptional standards of hygiene, offering flexible scheduling, and creating a welcoming environment where clients can truly unwind. Our convenient location with easy parking access makes your journey to wellness even smoother.
