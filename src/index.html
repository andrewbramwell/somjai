Featured Service

Thai Massage

Traditional Thai massage is a centuries-old healing practice that combines acupressure, gentle stretching, and rhythmic compression. This unique therapy is performed fully clothed on a comfortable mat or bed, where our therapist will guide you through a series of yoga-like stretches whilst applying precise pressure to energy (Sen) lines throughout your body.

Experience improved flexibility, reduced muscle tension, and enhanced energy flow throughout your body. This deeply relaxing yet energising treatment is particularly effective for those seeking relief from back pain, joint stiffness, headaches, and stress-related tension, helping to restore both physical and mental balance.

Aromatherapy Massage

Aromatherapy massage combines the gentle, flowing strokes of Swedish massage with the therapeutic properties of pure essential oils. Your treatment begins with a personalised blend of essential oils, chosen to address your specific needs—whether that's to reduce stress, boost energy, or promote better sleep.

This deeply relaxing massage uses light to medium pressure to enhance your body's natural healing processes while the carefully selected essential oils work through your sense of smell to affect mood and wellbeing. Perfect for those seeking relief from anxiety, emotional stress, or simply wanting to indulge in a luxuriously restorative experience.
